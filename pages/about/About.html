About Us

The School of Computing Science and Digital Media courses develop skills and expertise for a wide range of Computing technologies and target applications.

The School has an excellent reputation for its innovative and practical approaches to teaching and learning. For generations it has produced qualified professionals across a broad spectrum of technical careers with graduates in high profile, international roles. Our applied approach to the subject ensures our graduate employment rate remains one of the best in the UK. We have recently been recognised as the best new university in the UK for computing (Times Good University Guide 2009 and 2010).
